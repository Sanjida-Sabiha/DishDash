About the dish

Overview

Savor the simplicity and elegance of a Classic Margherita Pizza, a timeless favorite. Handcrafted with love, this pizza features a perfectly baked thin crust, topped with fresh tomato sauce, creamy mozzarella cheese, and fragrant basil leaves for a burst of authentic Italian flavors.

Pizza Dough: Hand-stretched and baked to golden perfection.
